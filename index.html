About Ubuntu Nexus

a youth-led initiative that works to prevent crime, exclusion, inequality and injustice and build peaceful communities through education, empowerment, advocacy, prevention, restoration and an Afrocentric lens rooted in the spirit of Ubuntu, “I am because we are.”

Our Programs & Pillars

Crime Prevention & Social Justice

Focusing on root causes, policy reform, and equitable systems to reduce crime and promote fairness.

YPS-PVE

Engaging youth in peacebuilding, leadership, and resilience-building to counter violent extremism.

Restorative Justice & Re-integration

Supporting healing and reintegration for individuals and communities affected by conflict or crime.

Education & Empowerment

Equipping communities with knowledge, tools, and opportunities for self-determination and growth.

Learn More About What We Do

Ubuntu Nexus is more than an initiative, it’s a movement. We believe in bottom-up change, where the voice of the community leads the way. From grassroots peace dialogues to youth re-integration programs, we aim to reframe justice from a human-centric perspective.
