About Ubuntu Justice and Peace Nexus (UJPN)

UJPN is dedicated to promoting justice, peace, and human dignity through community empowerment and advocacy. Our mission is to foster a society where everyone's rights are respected and upheld.

Through collaborative efforts, education, and sustainable initiatives, we aim to build inclusive communities grounded in Ubuntu - the spirit of humanity and togetherness.

Our Programs & Pillars

Crime Prevention & Social Justice

Focusing on root causes, policy reform, and equitable systems to reduce crime and promote fairness.

YPS-PVE

Engaging youth in peacebuilding, leadership, and resilience-building to counter violent extremism.

Restorative Justice & Re-integration

Supporting healing and reintegration for individuals and communities affected by conflict or crime.

Education & Empowerment

Equipping communities with knowledge, tools, and opportunities for self-determination and growth.

Learn More About What We Do

Ubuntu Justice and Peace Nexus (UJPN) is more than an initiative—it’s a movement. We believe in bottom-up change, where the voice of the community leads the way. From grassroots peace dialogues to youth rehabilitation programs, we aim to reframe justice from a human-centric perspective.
